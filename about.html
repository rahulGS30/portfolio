About

I am an engineer with a deep passion for drone technology and software development, blending hands-on experience with innovative solutions. As a certified drone pilot from MIT Chrompet, I've been engaged in the drone and robotics field since 11th grade, actively participating in competitions and conducting workshops through Team Tandem, a group I founded to share my knowledge. My journey includes designing and fabricating various drones, from automated quadcopters for delivery systems to underwater maneuvering drones and payload-dropping fixed-wing drones. I enjoy tackling complex challenges, such as balancing the center of gravity during drone assembly, and have also delved into software projects involving JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. My current work includes a final-year project on an accurate pollution monitoring system using a quadcopter, showcasing my skills in integrating hardware and software for impactful real-world applications. I thrive on continuous learning and bringing my technical expertise to life through innovative projects
